Design System
Color Palette
The color system relies on a strictly monochromatic core to emulate the darkness of a developing room or a digital terminal. Stark, luminous neon accents (Cyan and Red) are reserved purely for active data visualization and filtering states. Grey values (#A3A3A3) have been calibrated for optimal legibility against the #050505 deep background.

Typography
Typography is used to establish strict hierarchy and technical tone. Space Grotesk (Bold 700) provides brutalist, geometric anchors for primary titles. IBM Plex Mono carries the functional weight—its weight is set to Medium (500) at small sizes (11-13px) to ensure character strokes remain solid and highly legible against the dark void.

Images Style
The visual aesthetic centers around raw, unadulterated film scans. High contrast, deep crushed blacks, and naturally preserved film grain are encouraged. Images are embedded directly against the pure black background, utilizing negative space rather than heavy bounding boxes to draw focus.

Controls & States
Interactive components are designed to feel like physical switches or terminal commands. They rely on high-contrast changes (gray to white, or dark to neon) to clearly communicate active states.
Global Navigation Link
Main routing anchors. Active states feature a stark 1px white underline bridging the text.
ACTIVE STATE INACTIVE
Ghost Action Button
Used for secondary global actions (like Add Album). Inverts to solid white on hover for tactile feedback.
Map Filter Toggles
Custom checkboxes for the 3D map. Checked states illuminate with the system's neon accent colors (Cyan/Red).

Dropdown Select

Core Components & Layouts
The structural building blocks of the archive. Layouts prioritize extreme grid alignment and negative space over physical borders, creating an unbroken, cinematic viewing experience.
Gallery Focus Card (Home)
The hero element of the Personal Archive. Displays a 1:1 image format. When focused, a clean 1px solid white border illuminates the perimeter.
